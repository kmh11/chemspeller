About the website!

ChemSpeller is a website created by middle school student Kevin Higgs. With it you can spell one or multiple words with elements, subatomic particles, and units of measure. This allows all words to be spelled. You can click on an element to learn more about it using the first paragraph of the Wikipedia entry on that item. It also has animated backgrounds that appear randomly unless configured otherwise in the "Trick" field on the options screen.
